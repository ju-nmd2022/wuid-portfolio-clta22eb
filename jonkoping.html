✨About Jönköping:✨

Jönköping is one of the first cities in Sweden to be officially called a city and has been around since 1258. It is know for its close connection to second biggest lake in Sweden and being a center for many logistic warehouses. The most famous person from Jönköping is Agnetha Fältskog, who’s known for being one of the lead singers in ABBA.

What to see:

  • Vätterstranden
  • Gamla stan (smedjegatan)
  • Munksjön
  • Tändsticksområded
